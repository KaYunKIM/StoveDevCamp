- keyspace 조회

```
> DESC keyspaces
```

- keyspace 생성

```
> create keyspace lab with replication = {'class': 'SimpleStrategy', 'replication_factor':1};
```

- lab keyspace 사용하기

```
> use lab;
cqlsh:lab>
```
